How It Works - Step-by-Step Guide

Master SoTrade Pro with these simple steps to maximize your trading potential.

1

Identify the Main Trend

Open the Daily or 4H chart. Check the Dashboard: 4H and Daily must both show ▲ (Buy) or ▼ (Sell). TREND must display ▲ UPTREND or ▼ DOWNTREND.

2

Check EMA 200

Price above EMA 200 = Buy only. Price below EMA 200 = Sell only.

3

Find Entry on Lower Timeframe

Switch to 1H or 5m chart. Wait for ACTION to show ▲ BUY or ▼ SELL. Buy% or Sell% must be at least 60% (Strong).

4

Verify Conditions

RSI should not be Overbought (for Buy) or Oversold (for Sell). MACD must confirm the same direction.

5

Enter the Trade

Check Entry, Stop Loss, and Take Profit from the Dashboard. Never risk more than 1-2% of your portfolio per order.

6

Manage Your Order

Price hits TP = Close for profit. Price hits SL = Cut loss. ACTION changes to SIDEWAYS/WAIT = Consider closing.

Trading Tips & Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to maximize your success and minimize risks with SoTrade Pro.

Do's

  • Always check the main trend first
  • Only trade with the trend
  • Wait for clear signals (≥60%)
  • Always set a stop loss
  • Risk no more than 1-2% per order
  • Use at least 1:2 risk-reward ratio

Don'ts

  • Never trade against the trend
  • Never enter during sideways markets
  • Never enter when signal is BLOCKED
  • Never enter during WAIT status
  • Never use Martingale strategy
  • Never trade during major news events
